About us
East Africa Times is an independent newsroom covering politics, business, sport, and culture across the region. Our reporters work from Kigali, Nairobi, Kampala, and Dar es Salaam to bring accurate, on-the-ground reporting to readers across East Africa and beyond.
We believe in clear, careful journalism: every story is checked, every source is named where possible, and every correction is published openly.
